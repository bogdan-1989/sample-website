Owner, Christopher Benoit started his career in Southern Vermont, where he began learning the art of the dry stacked stone wall by one of the regions best stone masons at age 18. From there he took his education to Vermont Technical College to study Design and Horticulture and continued to work and learn for six years in Vermont. Since 2009, he and his family have called Denver home and look forward to working with you in the future.
